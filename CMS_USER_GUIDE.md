# CMS User Guide - Caspers Paintworks

Welcome to your Content Management System (CMS)! This guide will help you manage all the content on your website without needing technical knowledge.

## Accessing the CMS

1. Go to your website and log in to the admin panel
2. Navigate to `/admin/cms` or click the "CMS" link in the admin menu
3. You'll see the CMS Dashboard with all available modules

## CMS Dashboard

The main dashboard shows 6 content management modules:

- **Blog Posts** - Write and publish news articles
- **Testimonials** - Manage customer reviews
- **Services** - Edit your service offerings
- **Gallery** - Upload before/after project photos
- **Business Info** - Update contact details and company information
- **Analytics** - View website statistics (coming soon)

---

## 1. Blog Posts Manager

### Creating a New Blog Post

1. Click "Blog Posts" from the CMS Dashboard
2. Click "New Post" button
3. Fill in the following fields:
   - **Title** (required) - The main heading of your post
   - **Excerpt** - Brief summary shown in listings
   - **Content** - Full article text
   - **Category** - Organize posts (e.g., "news", "project-showcase", "tips")
   - **Featured** - Check to highlight this post
   - **Publish** - Check to make it live immediately

4. Click "Create Post"

### Editing a Blog Post

1. Find the post in the list
2. Click the edit icon (pencil)
3. Make your changes
4. Click "Update Post"

### Deleting a Blog Post

1. Find the post in the list
2. Click the delete icon (trash can)
3. Confirm deletion

### Publishing & Drafts

- **Published posts** show a visible eye icon and appear on your website
- **Draft posts** show a hidden eye icon and only you can see them
- You can publish drafts anytime by editing and checking "Publish"

## 2. Testimonials Manager

### Adding a Customer Review

1. Click "Testimonials" from the CMS Dashboard
2. Click "New Testimonial"
3. Fill in:
   - **Customer Name** (required) - Who gave the review
   - **Title/Vehicle** - Optional (e.g., "1967 Mustang Owner")
   - **Quote** (required) - The actual testimonial text
   - **Rating** - 1-5 stars
   - **Approve for Display** - Check to show on website
   - **Featured** - Check to highlight this testimonial

4. Click "Add Testimonial"

### Managing Testimonials

- **Approve/Unapprove** - Click the checkmark icon to control visibility
- **Edit** - Click the pencil icon to make changes
- **Delete** - Click the trash icon to remove

### Best Practices

- Only approve testimonials that are appropriate for your website
- Feature your best reviews to highlight customer satisfaction
- Respond to feedback and use it to improve your services

---

## 3. Services Manager

### Creating a New Service

1. Click "Services" from the CMS Dashboard
2. Click "New Service"
3. Fill in:
   - **Service Name** (required) - e.g., "Custom Paint"
   - **Short Description** - Brief overview for cards
   - **Full Description** (required) - Detailed explanation
   - **Starting Price** - Base price in dollars
   - **Price Description** - How to display pricing (e.g., "Starting at $500")
   - **Turnaround Time** - How long it takes (e.g., "2-4 weeks")
   - **Service Features** - Add specific benefits/features

4. Click "Create Service"

### Adding Features

1. Type a feature in the text field (e.g., "High-quality materials")
2. Click "Add" or press Enter
3. Features appear as colored tags
4. Click the Ã— on a tag to remove it

### Editing Services

1. Find the service in the list
2. Click the edit icon
3. Update information
4. Click "Update Service"

### Pricing Tips

- Be clear about what's included in your starting price
- Use "Price Description" to explain pricing structure
- Update prices regularly to stay competitive

## 4. Gallery Manager

### Uploading Project Photos

1. Click "Gallery" from the CMS Dashboard
2. Click "New Gallery Item"
3. Fill in:
   - **Project Title** (required) - Name of the project
   - **Description** - Details about the work
   - **Category** - Type of work (Custom Paint, Restoration, etc.)
   - **Before Image URL** (required) - Link to before photo
   - **After Image URL** (required) - Link to after photo
   - **Featured** - Check to highlight this project

4. Click "Add Item"

### Image URLs

You need to provide image URLs. You can:
- Use images from your cloud storage (Google Drive, Dropbox, etc.)
- Upload to a free image hosting service (Imgur, Cloudinary, etc.)
- Use your website's image storage

### Managing Gallery Items

- **Edit** - Click pencil to update project details
- **Delete** - Click trash to remove
- **Featured Projects** - Appear prominently on your website

### Gallery Tips

- Use high-quality, well-lit photos
- Ensure before/after photos are taken from similar angles
- Include detailed descriptions of the work done
- Feature your best work to attract customers

---

## 5. Business Information Manager

### Updating Contact Details

1. Click "Business Info" from the CMS Dashboard
2. Update these fields:
   - **Business Name** - Your company name
   - **Phone** - Customer contact number
   - **Email** - Business email address
   - **Address** - Full business address

3. Click "Save Changes"

### Setting Business Hours

1. For each day of the week, set:
   - **Opening Time** - When you open (e.g., 09:00)
   - **Closing Time** - When you close (e.g., 17:00)

2. Hours are displayed in 24-hour format
3. Click "Save Changes"

### Social Media Links

Add links to your social media profiles:
- Instagram
- Facebook
- Twitter
- YouTube

These links appear in your website footer and contact pages.

### About & Mission

1. **About Your Business** - Tell customers your story
2. **Mission Statement** - Explain your business values

These sections help build trust with potential customers.

### Business Statistics

Display impressive numbers:
- **Years in Business** - How long you've been operating
- **Projects Completed** - Total number of projects
- **Satisfaction Rate** - Customer satisfaction percentage (0-100)

These stats appear on your website to build credibility.

### Saving Changes

Always click "Save Changes" after making updates. You'll see a confirmation message.

## Troubleshooting

### I can't log in to the CMS

- Make sure you're using the correct admin password
- Clear your browser cache and try again
- Contact your website administrator

### Changes aren't showing on the website

- Make sure you clicked "Save Changes" or "Create/Update"
- Wait a few seconds for changes to appear
- Refresh your browser (Ctrl+F5 or Cmd+Shift+R)

### Image URLs aren't working

- Check that the URL is complete (starts with http:// or https://)
- Make sure the image file still exists at that URL
- Try uploading to a different image hosting service

### I accidentally deleted something

- Contact your website administrator to restore from backup
- Be careful when clicking delete buttons
